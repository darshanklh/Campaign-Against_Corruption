Take the Pledge

Building Integrity: The Moral Foundation

Topic: Our Core Mission Pillar

Transparency exposes corruption, but **integrity** prevents it. A system is only as honest as the people within it. Our mission is to foster a culture where integrity is not just a virtue, but the default standard for every citizen and public servant.

The Citizen's Integrity Pledge

The Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) promotes an "Integrity Pledge" that we fully endorse. It is a simple, powerful commitment that every Indian should take to heart. Your "pledge" on our homepage is inspired by this.

I believe that corruption has been one of the major obstacles to the economic, political and social progress of our country. I believe that all stakeholders... need to work together to eradicate corruption.

I, therefore, pledge:
• To follow probity and rule of law in all walks of life;
• To neither take nor offer bribe;
• To perform all tasks in an honest and transparent manner;
• To act in public interest;
• To lead by example exhibiting integrity in personal behavior;
• To report any incident of corruption to the appropriate agency.

Case Studies in Courage: The Whistleblowers

Integrity is not easy. It often comes at a great personal cost. We honor the heroes who gave their lives to expose the truth—their stories are the "proof" that one person's integrity can shake the most corrupt systems.

Satyendra Dubey

An IIT engineer working on the Golden Quadrilateral highway project. He was murdered in 2003 after he wrote directly to the PMO exposing financial irregularities and poor-quality construction by contractors.

Shanmugam Manjunath

An IIM graduate and IOC officer. He was murdered in 2005 for sealing a corrupt petrol pump that was selling adulterated fuel. His murder led to a nationwide movement for justice.

Our Goal

We cannot let these sacrifices be in vain. Our mission is to build a society that protects its whistleblowers and celebrates honesty. We advocate for stronger ethics education in schools, mandatory integrity training in public service, and a social movement where citizens actively refuse to participate in corruption, starting with the simple pledge: **"I will neither take nor offer a bribe."**
