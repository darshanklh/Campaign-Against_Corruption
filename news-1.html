Haryana's 'Antyodaya SARAL' Portal Wins National E-Governance Award

Published on October 30, 2025 | By Campaign Staff

In a major boost for digital transparency, Haryana's 'Antyodaya SARAL' portal has been awarded the 'Gold Award' at the National Conference on e-Governance. This platform is being celebrated as a model for other states in its mission to create a "faceless, paperless, and cashless" service delivery system.

The portal acts as a single, unified platform for citizens to access and apply for government services, effectively minimizing the direct contact between citizens and government officials. This reduction in physical interaction is a cornerstone of anti-corruption efforts, as it removes opportunities for bribery and discretionary treatment.
