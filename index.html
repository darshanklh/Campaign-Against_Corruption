Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main goal of this campaign?

Our main goal is to promote transparency and integrity in all sectors of society. We aim to educate citizens about their rights (like RTI) and empower them to say "no" to corruption.

Is this campaign affiliated with any political party?

No. We are a non-partisan, citizen-led movement. Our only allegiance is to the principles of honesty, integrity, and a corruption-free India.

I am just one person. What can I do?

You can do a lot! Start by taking the Integrity Pledge. Educate yourself about your rights. Never give a bribe, and report corruption when you see it. Talk to your friends and family. Every single person who stands up for honesty makes a huge difference.

I witnessed corruption. What should I do?

If it is safe to do so, document it. You can then report it to the appropriate authorities, such as the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) or your state's Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB). We have links in our "Report" section.

The Integrity Pledge

I, as a citizen of India, solemnly pledge:

  • I will not give or accept a bribe.
  • I will perform all my duties with honesty and transparency.
  • I will hold myself and others accountable for our actions.
  • I will be the change I want to see for a corruption-free India.
