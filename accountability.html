Ensuring Accountability: Justice Must Be Served

Topic: Our Core Mission Pillar

Transparency and integrity are meaningless without accountability. When corruption is exposed, there must be swift, certain, and impartial consequences. This pillar of our mission focuses on strengthening the institutions that act as the public's watchdogs and ensuring that justice is not just promised, but delivered.

The Data: The Accountability Gap

The hard truth is that India has a serious "accountability gap." While arrests are made, convictions remain alarmingly low. This impunity is what emboldens corrupt officials. The data from the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) "Crime in India" report paints a stark picture.

976

Persons convicted in corruption cases in 2023.

2,010

Persons acquitted (found not guilty) in corruption cases in 2023.

11,300+

Corruption cases pending in court at the end of 2023, creating a massive backlog.

Our Institutional Watchdogs

India is not without powerful anti-corruption bodies. Our work is to support them, demand they remain independent, and call for the reforms needed to make them more effective.

  • The Lokpal and Lokayuktas: The Lokpal is the national anti-corruption ombudsman with jurisdiction to investigate corruption allegations against public officials, including the Prime Minister. The Lokayuktas perform this function at the state level. We advocate for strengthening these bodies and ensuring they are fully funded and staffed.
  • Central Vigilance Commission (CVC): The CVC is the apex integrity institution. While its role is primarily advisory, it plays a crucial part in overseeing vigilance administration. A strong CVC is essential for preventing corruption before it starts.

Our Goal

We demand a time-bound justice system for corruption cases. A case that drags on for a decade is justice denied. Our campaign advocates for:

  1. Special "Fast-Track" Courts dedicated solely to corruption cases.
  2. Strengthening the independence of the Lokpal, CVC, and state ACBs.
  3. A higher conviction rate, driven by better, technology-assisted investigations and independent prosecution.

Accountability is the final, critical piece of the puzzle. It is the proof that our society will not tolerate those who betray the public trust.
